Diary 1 — June 27

2026_05_30_D1_May25 / D1_040_June27_IMG_0009.CR3

At 5.25 we pulled out,
arriving at the barracks
at 6.35(?). I stood the walk
well, though. I carried
two guns the last half
of the way. We were glad
to crawl into our bunks
that night.

Thursday June 27

We had gas
mask drill this
morning. In the afternoon
the company was sized
up. I was placed in
charge of the second squad.
In the evening I went
to the infirmary for another
vaccination. That makes
the fourth.

Friday June 28

The first platoon
was moved into
the lower barracks today.
