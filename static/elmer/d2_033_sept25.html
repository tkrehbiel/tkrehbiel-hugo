Diary 2 — Sept 25

came that we were not to
move that night. S?? it
[?] the good night’s sleep.
Thursday Sept to fall in. We
marched out of the
woods and formed the
battalion in an open field
then the real march(?) began.
It was very dark, and
as we kept beneath the
trees it made it darker.
It was an awful march.
We were the last company
in line, so we were halted
every twenty yards, then
double timed to catch up.
We followed a cart track
about half a mile, then
cut through the woods.
