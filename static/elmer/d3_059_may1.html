Diary 3 — May 1

2026_06_19_D3_Apr3 / D3_059_May1_IMG_0009.CR3

to do but kill time.

Thursday May 1

On guard today.
Sounds(?) terrific
but it wasn’t. I did the
same amount of nothing,
only I had a belt on.

Friday May 2

We learn that
we are to sail between
the 9th and the 17th. Speed
the day.

Saturday May 3

Took a long
walk today. This
country is vastly different
from Haute Marne. Here 3/4 of
the farm is vinyard [sic]. The
rest seems to be cow feed.
This is cut green and wheeled(?)
in(?) every day. It is wonder-
fully beautiful, but sadly(?)
disfigured by the trees. They(?)
have the branches lopped
off every year leaving an
ugly(?), unsightly stump.

Sunday May 4

Did nothing.
