Diary 1 — July 11

2026_05_30_D1_May25 / D1_050_July11_IMG_0019.CR3

When we were standing
reveille one fellow got sea-
sick. It wasn’t pleasant
to watch him.

I was not sick all
day, probably because I
didn’t eat supper the
night before or breakfast
in the morning. But I
ate a heavy dinner.

We saw a school of
porpoises, three blackfish(?)
and a sea turtle. That
is all the signs of life
that we have seen, outside
of our company.

[New pencil.]
