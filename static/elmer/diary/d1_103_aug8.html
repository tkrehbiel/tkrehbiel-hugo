Thursday Aug 8

We went on the
range today. I wont
say much about it, because
I never shot so rotten in
my life.

I was in the pit in
the morning and got a taste
of bullets overhead(?). One ri-
chochet came so close I felt
the wind of it, but even so
it was two feet overhead.

Friday Aug 9

day. I dont know
whether it is the better food(?)
