I can’t say that I liked
the visit to town. It was
too short. I went to Keith’s(?)
and the Columbia, and ate
till it hurt. That was all
my vacation.

[Keith’s Theatre was one of Baltimore’s premier vaudeville theaters, part of the famous B. F. Keith theatrical circuit. Located on Lexington Street, it hosted a changing lineup of comedians, singers, musicians, and variety acts and was a popular destination for soldiers stationed at nearby Camp Meade during World War I.]

May 19 Sunday

A Sunday just as
slow as they gen-
erally are. But I’ve found
a bright spot in the library.
There is good reading matter
there. I almost missed
retreat on that account,
I didn’t get there till
after roll call.
