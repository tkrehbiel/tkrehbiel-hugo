We soon pulled out, ran
a little way down the track,
then branched off and ran
on for an hour. We came to
a stop at a little station,
and got the welcome order to
detrain. But my squad
managed to get on fatigue
again, so we dropped our
packs alongside the track and
helped unload provisions. We
were there till four oclock.
We did manage to have
our packs and barrack
bags sent up on a truck; all
the other men had to carry their
packs up, then come back
and tote up their bags.
