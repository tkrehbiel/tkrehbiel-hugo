Outside of that we have a
good boat. She is a wide and
roomy old cargo tub, but she(?) is(?)
good and clean, much cleaner
than the Agamemnon. Here
decks are all of metal, making
her easy to keep clean. There
is of course, little room in
the berthing spaces, but we
are not restricted(?) at all, so
we are well off.

Sunday May 18

Rather calmer
today. The ship
riding much easier. I found
out that there is no library
aboard.
