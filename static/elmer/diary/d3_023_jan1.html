Wednesday Jan 1 1919

In bed all day.
I felt normal
in the morning but my fever
began to mount in the after-
noon. So I had to stay in bed.

Thursday Jan 2

In bed all day.
In the evening I
was transferred to ward 10.

Friday Jan 3

I have a queer sick-
ness. In the morning
I feel good. Then about noon
my fever begins and mounts(?)
steadily. About 10 oclock at
night I wonder the blankets
dont scortch [sic]. Then I begin to
sweat; my fever goes down and
I lay the rest of the night and
shiver in my wet bed.

Saturday Jan 4

Nothing new.

Sunday Jan 5

Ditto.

Monday Jan 6

Ditto.

Tuesday Jan 7

My fever less(?), be-
lieve I’m recovering(?).
