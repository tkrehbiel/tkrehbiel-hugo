Sunday Feb 9

Visited Barde(?)
vauix(?) again [?]
tried to get some yarn(?) but

[See also: Elmer’s February 9 letter.]

Monday Feb 10

More drill. That
orchestra(?) is falling
through.

Tuesday Feb 11

We missed our
customary maneuver
today. I dont know why.

Wednesday Feb 12

We had no(?)
outside drill today
it was too cold, and they
were actually merciful(?).

Thursday Feb 13

A maneuver to-
day, this one,
for a change, interesting.
The day was beautiful,
just cold enough to make(?)
us good and [?] [1:snappy.]

Friday Feb 14

February half
gone, and still no
sign of a move.

Saturday Feb 15

On guard today
so I got out of
inspection again.

Sunday Feb 16

Visited Heippes(?)
today for a change.
