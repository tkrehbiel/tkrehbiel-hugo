it was the best coffee I have
had since I left home, so I
forgive them. It had real
milk and sugar in it! It
was served to us by the
Red Cross.

After “supper” we were
told to be ready to detrain
one hour after the train
left here. But we were
already wise, so we made
complete preparations to sleep.
We spread a layer on the
bottom, then layed [sic] planks
across the benches and
another layer on that. I
was in the lower deck. But
I slept fairly well. At
2 o’clock a detail left
the car to draw rations
