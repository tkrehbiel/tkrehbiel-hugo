Sunday Apr 20

Easter Sunday. Went
to services at the Y, then(?)
spent the rest of the day walking.

In a woods nearby I
saw an unusual geological
formation. This country is all
underlaid with soft limestone.
I saw a sink. There was a
steeply conical hole through
the soil, say 30 feet deep, then
at the bottom a round well(?)
worn into the rock, about 6 feet
across and ten feet deep.
The bottom was of sand. A little
creek or stream flowed into the
sink and disappeared under-
ground.

Monday Apr 21

The awful day has
come; I am reduced
to duty. I had to go out
to drill. It wasn’t much,
only an hour and a half, but
it humbled my pride.

[See also: Elmer’s April 21 letter.]

Tuesday Apr 22

Drill again.

Wednesday Apr 23

In charge of quarters(?)
today, a soft job.
