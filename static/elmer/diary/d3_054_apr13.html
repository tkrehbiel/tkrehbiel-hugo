Diary 3 — Apr 13

The hike back was awful, for
the weather got steadily worse.
We got in at 4 oclock, very tired
and very, very hungry.

Sunday Apr 13

Got a detail today
for missing formation
on Saturday. Damn that ser-
geant.

Monday Apr 14

School is finee [sic] till
we are again settled
down, but we wont mention it
to anyone or we’ll have to drill.

Tuesday Apr 15

The company drilling
a little, me ducking
as usual. Took a walk to the
next village south. Nothing there.

[See also: Elmer’s April 15 letter.]

Wednesday Apr 16

Nothing doing,
a bad day.

Thursday Apr 17

We hear that orders
are changed again. We
will move Tuesday of next week,
going to Renaucourt(?) and from
there to Nantes on the coast.

[See also: Elmer’s April 17 letter.]

Friday Apr 18

Business as usual, no-
thing new.

Saturday Apr 19

Cleaned up for inspection,
but it didn’t come off.
Took the rest of the day off.
