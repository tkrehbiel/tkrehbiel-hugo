as it was at first. I
even get some sleep in
my bunk. The days pass
easily with reading
and talking, the only
trouble is that mealtime
does not come around
often enough.

Some of the men
say they saw a gull
today. I didn’t see it,
all I saw was another
shark.

In the evening I
got a dose of seasickness.
I didn’t vomit, probably
because I didn’t have
anything in my stomach,
but I certainly(?) felt miser-
able.
