They all told great tales of their(?)
sections of the country.

Saturday Oct 5

The same as
yesterday.
The doctor [?] my
wound will heal in 10 days.

[It’s not clear what kind of wound Elmer received. He only mentioned he got “quite a bad cut” on his finger. I can’t see how a cut finger would require an evacuation and take 10 days to heal, but I suppose it’s worth remembering that medical technology was quite different in 1918.]

[It occurs to me to wonder how Elmer is able to keep writing with what I have to assume is a heavily bandaged index finger.]

Sunday Oct 6

Laying in bed yet.
I wish they would
hurry up with that uniform.

Monday Oct 7

Ditto. One fellow
in our room had been
knocked on the helmet by a piece
of shell. When he came to, he
could not see. They sent him
back, and gradually the
sight came back in his right
eye. But the left eye turned
