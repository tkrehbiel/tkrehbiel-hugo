Wednesday May 2

The sea is
better today.
The waves are about 10 feet high,
enough to bob the ship about
pretty badly.

Thursday May 22

The sea is now
calm and the
ship is running pretty good.
The weather is damp(?) and
rainy however.

They have distributed
some magazines(?) and papers
now.

Friday May 23

Nothing to report.
The sea very calm
and the sky clear. Rather
cold yet.

Saturday May 24

Had a rifle in-
spection today
and got my name taken for a
dirty gun. Guess I’ll stand
guard.
