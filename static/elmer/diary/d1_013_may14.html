we went to a big open am-
pitheater [sic], where we had a sort
of jubilee. We sang songs,
listened to the band, had
bonfires, etc. The older men
say that this means that a
big bunch is going off soon.

May 14 Tuesday

Room Orderly today.
I swept the floor,
about 3 hours work in all,
then did bunk fatigue for the
rest of the day.

May 15 Wednesday

We had bacon for
breakfast and I split
a big piece one of my teeth [sic].
I’ll get a chance to see how
an army dentist works.

We had the usual hike
at about 8.00 covering 5 miles
and returning at 11.00. We
visited the bombing school
