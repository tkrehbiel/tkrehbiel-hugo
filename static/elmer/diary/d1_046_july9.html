The feeding(?) here
is a revelation to me.
It is built for speed
and minimum stowing
space. You grab your
grub on the run, eat it
standing at tables (12” wide)
swing [swung?] from the ceiling, then
wash your dishes on the
run. The whole process
takes 10 minutes.

But I’ve had awful poor
beds for the last four or
five nights; I’m going to
see if I can rest on this
piece of canvas(?).
