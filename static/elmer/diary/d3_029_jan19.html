Sunday Jan 19

We went on pass
today, to the
next(?) town, Benatvauix(?).
Of course it is nothing
much; But they do have
a very nice church, and
a sculpture of the 14(?)
stations to the cross, that
is fine work.

I met Andrew Gross,
from Douglas, there.

Monday Jan 20

We went out to
drill this morning
And it is killing(?) work
for me. If they hadn’t(?)
took [sic] us in at 10 oclock, I
wouldn’t have been able to
stick it out.

Tuesday Jan 21

the afternoon(?)
we play games. I played(?)
volley ball today, and
