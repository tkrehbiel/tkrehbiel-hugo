May 23 Thursday

Got the company
sized at last into
properly graded ranks. I
was assign to No 3, rear
rank, First squad, But I
haven’t drilled there yet. I’m
still assistant drill master.

In the afternoon we
got a grand treat. We were
taken to the base hospital
for a mental examination. It
was a joke to some and a
horror to others. From the
remarks passed later I think
the whole company will be
classed as hopeless idiots.
