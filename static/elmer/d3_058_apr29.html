Diary 3 — Apr 29

2026_06_19_D3_Apr3 / D3_058_Apr29_IMG_0008.CR3

Nantes. Our train switched(?)
off and we got out about 25
kilos away.

We were marched off. Co H
separated from the rest of
the battalion and entered(?)
the town of Mouzillon(?).

After what we have known,
this town is a treat. It is
beautifully clean and neat(?),
there are no manure piles, and
the people are well dressed and
clean. The billets are good, we
are in a theater.

The church here is a beauty(?).
It is one of those that you
see in travel books, a real
Gothic cathedral. It is built(?)
of white stone, and is a dom-
inant landmark for miles
around.

Tuesday Apr 29

My 24th birthday
and my 1st year
in the army. Many happy
returns of the first, three
groans for the second.

Wednesday Apr 30

Well settled
down. Nothing
