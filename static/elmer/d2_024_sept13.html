Friday Sept 13

The morning passed
without event, except
for one thing; through the YMCA
we got candy, manufactured
by the QMC(?) for the AEF. It
was certainly a treat to get
genuine American candy once
again. Also, I spent the
morning in eating. I expected
to go to the Front, so I de-
termined to get rid of all
my money. We bought up
everything eatable in town,
and besides, ate up a good
many rations that could not(?)
