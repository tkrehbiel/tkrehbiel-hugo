We are beginning to
get into the mud(?) here. And
no wonder! They tell me that
it was rained 6 times in 2
days. I’m beginning to think
that the raincoat is mightier
than the sword, also. The
soil is white and chalky,
and the mud seems made
apurpose for messing up kahki [sic]
uniforms.

Wednesday July 24

When we woke
up we were still
laying at the same dump(?).
The first battalion, though,
has pulled out.

We had our cup of coffee
and slice of bread again,
then loafed around on the
train.
