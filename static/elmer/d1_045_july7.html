Diary 1 — July 7

2026_05_30_D1_May25 / D1_045_July7_IMG_0014.CR3

This troop movement is
of course very secret.
But at Philadelphia,
(I was guard outside), a
girl came up to me and
said “Which coach is
Mr. – [sic] in?” And one
of the trainmen said, “I
guess Camp Meade is
just about empty now, isn’t
it?”

The accommodations
on the ship are a realyzation [sic]
of my worst fears. The bunks
are three deep, each two
feet wide and there is
an 18 inch aisle around
every pair of bunks.
We are on the fourth deck,
just below water level,
where a torpedo would
