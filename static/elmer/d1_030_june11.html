Tuesday June 11

We drilled with
the old company
and I was surprised
at them. They are rotten.
We were to spend from
7 to 12 PM in the trenches
so we got our supper at
5 PM. At 6 we were marched
off. We were in luck
this time. A rain
the night before had
soaked up the ground,
and it was mostly sand
at that. So we got
along well for our shift.
I stood the work well too.
