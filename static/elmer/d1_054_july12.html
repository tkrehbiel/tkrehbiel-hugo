Diary 1 — July 12

2026_05_30_D1_May25 / D1_054_July12_IMG_0023.CR3

fast asleep. I didn’t wake
up till the lights were
turned on in the morning.

Early this morning I
deliberately broke one of
the rules. I took a bath.
I decided that I’d just
as soon be under arrest
as stay where I was so
I took a chance. And
I’m going to do it again.

[New pencil.] Late in the evening
I was leaning over
the rail when I saw
a flock of white birds
out on the water. I thought
it queer that they should
be there days out
from land, when it
struck me that they
were flying fishes!
