Diary 2 — Sept 11

2026_06_02_D2_Sept10 / D2_021_Sept11_IMG_0003.CR3

1 oclock. We passed through
a very pretty country, hilly,
as ever. The rocks, though,
seemed to be lacking.

We had a good feed in the
car(?), then tried to go to sleep.
But it was impossible, there
were too many of us. We could
only sit up against the wall.
So we had another miserable
night.

Wednesday Sept 11

We had break-
fast on the cars, then
at about 9 oclock we got orders
to detrain, about 4 miles
beyond Barle Duc. We de-
camped in a field of mud
