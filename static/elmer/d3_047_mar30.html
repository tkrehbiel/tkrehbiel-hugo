Longville proved to be a

Sunday Mar 30

Today it was snowing(?)
gently when we got up.
We had breakfast, then I
put my dry things into my
overcoat, as yesterday, and
put on my wet ones, intend(?)-
ing to keep my dry stuff
till the afternoon.

The hike was easy
today. We had one long,
very long, uphill climb to
start, then the road was
good the rest of the day.
Everyone was cheerful again
too, that helped wonderfully.
The country was mostly woods
