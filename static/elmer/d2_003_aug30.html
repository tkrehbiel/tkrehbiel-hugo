Diary 2 — Aug 30

2026_06_01_D2_Aug25 / D2_003_Aug30_IMG_0006.CR3

this morning, the first platoon
defending and the other three
attacking. I was one of the
casualties.

In the afternoon we had
grenade throwing. Our drills
are certainly getting easy.

I went out where the grenades
landed and picked up a hand-
ful of fragments, any one of which
would have passed through a
ladies ring. Yet in spite of
this fragmentation, two trees
within twenty feet of 80 bombs
were not touched. That is
reassuring to us.

Friday Aug 30

[New pencil.] The usual drills,
nothing new of any kind.
