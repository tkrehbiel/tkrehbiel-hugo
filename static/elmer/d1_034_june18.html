June 18 Tuesday

We had the usual
close order drill in
the morning. In the after-
noon we went onto the
assault course. We had
our first rifle firing. I
made a perfect score; my
rifle shoots beautifully.

June 19 Wednesday

We had battle
practise [sic] in the
morning. It was not on
a very extended scale but
it was enough to convince
us that we know very little
about it.
