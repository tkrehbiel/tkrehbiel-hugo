Diary 2 — Aug 25

2026_06_01_D2_Aug25 / D2_001_Aug25_IMG_0003.CR3

[Sunday] Aug 25

[I] spent the morn(?)-
ing in writing. In
the afternoon I was [new pencil] on pass in
the afternoon. I just wandered
around the country. It is beaut-
iful. I should want nothing
better than to loaf away a
summer here. I walked away(?)
myself tired. Then, instead
of sleeping, we were taken out
on a compass problem. It was
as tough as a regular day.

Monday Aug 26

We went out on the
rifle range again.
I am learning my rifle very
well. We didn’t get back till
8 o’clock, then we did get a
good meal(?) for a wonder.
