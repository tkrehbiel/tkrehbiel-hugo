Monday July 29

Today we had a taste
of intensive training.
It was awful work. I under-
stand now why a courtmartial
never gives hard labor. We drilled
continuously, without a single
fall out, until 12 oclock. At
1.20 we fell out again and
drilled till 4.30. This is to
be our usual day.

Tuesday July 30

We had a hike
today. I enjoyed
hikes at Camp Meade but in
these hard roads its very
different. My feet ached
badly.

We walked out about
three miles then came back
in formation. Our platoon
