Diary 1 — Apr 30

we would get beans three
times a day, so I almost
died when they were dished
out. I wanted to quit right
there. The bread here is good; it
is made of straight wheat flour.

In the evening we had a
short walk, about 1 hour and
we covered about 2 miles.

Supper; beef potpie, th? b??t [the best?]
meat dish I ever ate.

We got our vaccination and
the first typhoid injection after
supper. I didn’t think either
were bad.

Apr 30 Tuesday

The bunch(?) is pretty
quiet this morning. My
left arm is so stiff I can’t
lift it at all. We had no
drilling to do, everyone stayed
in(?) barracks, most of them
