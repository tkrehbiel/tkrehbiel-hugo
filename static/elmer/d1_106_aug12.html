Diary 1 — Aug 12

2026_05_31_D1_July13 / D1_106_Aug12_IMG_0051.CR3

We got in at 1 oclock, ate
mess immediately and went
right out to drill.

Tuesday Aug 12

We hiked over
to the divisional
trenches this morning and
layed [sic] out a system of trenches
for a strong point. It was
not heavy work.

In the afternoon we
were at drill again, all ex-
tended order. I acted sergeant.

Wednesday Aug 14

We certainly
had enough of
walking today. We went
to the top of “Mont Blanc”
in the morning for more
layout out of positions.

there. The limestone(?) lays
