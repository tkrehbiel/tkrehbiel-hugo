Diary 3 — Mar 18

2026_06_18_D3_Feb28 / D3_042_Mar18_IMG_0007.CR3

Tuesday Mar 18

Raining so hard
that even the com-
pany didn’t go out.

Wednesday Mar 19

The company is(?)
out on the range.
Me doing nothing.

Thursday Mar 20

Today I got my
first letter from home
for three months. It explained
why I hadn’t gotten any before.
They thought I was coming over,
so they didn’t bother to write.

Friday Mar 21

Nothing doing, again(?).
We ran out of firewood(?)
so we sat miserably around
the cold stove.

Saturday Mar 22

I stood inspection
today just for fun.
And I came within an inch
of being nabbed. Never
again.

Sunday Mar 23

I asked for a pass
today, and didnt
get it. So I went anyway
with Brady to Benoitaux(?).
We didn’t get caught.
