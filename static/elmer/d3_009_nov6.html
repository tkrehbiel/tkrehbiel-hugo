Wednesday Nov 6

This morning a
roll was made and
the company reorganized. There
are 7 squads left! I was put
in charge of one. There was only
one sergeant left and one officer.
One curious result, was that we
now had more to eat than I
ever saw in my life. We still
drew rations for a full company,
and so there was more than
anyone could handle.

Tonight there is to be another
raid and this time I’ll go along.

Thursday Nov 7

The company assem-
bled at midnight(?).
Just before starting I heard
the lieutenant calling my name.
I responded and he ordered
me to stay with the packs!
He said there was to be a long
march and probably tough work
and I wouldn’t be able(?) to stand
it.
