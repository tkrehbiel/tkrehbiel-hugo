That was a terrific
walk. It was probably
three miles in all, but it
was a merciless job for
men who had done nothing
for ten days. I soon began
to wonder where all the
beef that I had been
stowing away on the boat
had gotten to.

We got out to where we
were going at last. It
was only a bare field though.
The American Army
has been coming over so
fast that space can’t be
made for it; so we have
to bunk in the open for
