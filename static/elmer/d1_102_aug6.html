Tuesday Aug 6

[New pencil.]

I got my first
letter from home
today. It wasn’t long, but
it certainly made me home-
sick. I felt rotten when I
didn’t get one before, but I
felt still worse when I got one.

It rained Tuesday
night, beating directly into
the windows. My bedding
was soaked, so I spent
a bad night.
