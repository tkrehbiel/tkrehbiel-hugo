Elmer's WW1 Diaries

Co. H, 316th Infantry · American Expeditionary Forces · 1918–1919

This site presents the wartime diaries of Elmer H. Krehbiel, who served with Company H, 316th Infantry, in the American Expeditionary Forces in France during the First World War. Each entry below links to a single diary page, showing a photograph of the original alongside a transcription of Elmer's handwriting. Skip to September 24 for the Meusse-Argonne offensive.

The diary pages on this site were photographed and transcribed in 2026 by Thomas Krehbiel.
