June 2 Sunday

Off on pass. Got
in about 15 min-
utes late. The car’s fault.

June 3 Monday

The usual drills.
We have bayonet
drill every afternoon now.
It is strenuous work, but
today was fairly cool(?).
An aeroplane, one of the
postplanes, landed on the
drill field. We double timed
over to it and fell out to
take a look at it. It was
a Curtis plane, 2 passenger.
It was a beauty.
