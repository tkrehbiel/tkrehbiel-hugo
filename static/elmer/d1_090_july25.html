flour. [New pencil.] There are
camps all over this section, so
the trucks are pretty busy
all the time.

We had a rather poor
mess, then I arranged
my bunk as best I could.
I had to sleep in my
clothes again.

Thursday July 25

This morning
we got out to
drill, bright and early.
We only drilled in the road,
though, so we couldn’t do
much beside Manual of
Arms and To the Rear. We
just had an opportunity
to see how rotten we
were.

In the afternoon they
took us up to a high hill.
