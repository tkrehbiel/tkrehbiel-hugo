Diary 1 — July 26

Then I undressed for a
good nights sleep. Soon my
feet felt cold, so I got up
and put on some socks. Then
I put on a sweater. Late
at night I found it necess-
ary to put on my breeches
and before morning I had
to put on my blouse.

I had a severe back-
ache too, that prevented
me from sleeping.

Friday July 26

Today we were
told to fall out in
fatigue uniforms. We started
in by policing up our barracks,
billets, they are now. We
got a bushel of dirt from
our room. We had to
move a lumberpile too.
