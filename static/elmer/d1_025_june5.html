evening our new Enfields
were issued. They were
coated 1/4 inch deep with
cosmoline, the messiest
grease I ever saw. I have
worked on mine for three
hours and it is not clean
yet.

June 5 Wednesday

Instead of our
usual hike, we
went on fatigue this
morning. They took us
out in the woods about
a mile from here and
we cut sticks to make
bayonet dummies with.
I was nominally(?) in
charge of a gang. The
first hour we worked
very consciously(?), the
second hour we rested
