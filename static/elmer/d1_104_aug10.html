Saturday Aug 10

We went to the
divisional trenches
in the morning and dug
in with our toy tools. The
French people gave us the
roughest and most valueless(?)
piece of ground in the country.
Digging in was a matter
of finding the joints in the
rock and prying them up
without breaking the pick. I
raised a blister in half an
hour.

In the afternoon we had
the usual drill, on a level
field just outside of town.
