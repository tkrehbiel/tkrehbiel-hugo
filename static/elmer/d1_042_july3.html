Diary 1 — July 3

Wednesday July 3

Inspection
in the morning,
in the evening gas drill
and a trip to the gas house.
They locked us in a chamber
of, first lactrimatory [sic] gas,
then chlorine gas. There
was no faking, it was the
real stuff. But our masks
protected us, there was
nothing unpleasant, except
the stiffiness [sic] of the masks.
The walk over and back
was the worst of it; three
miles each way of six
inch deep dust.

Thursday July 4

An inspection
today, and a
very little drill. Most
of the fellows had visitors.
There was no blowout
