And we went at top speed too.
I thought we were in more
danger from the driver than
from shells. The drivers, I
should add, were Japs. We
saw practically nothing on
the trip, for it was very dark.

Saturday Sept 14

Just about dawn we
were unloaded. We
walked a mile or so, to a
village called Blercourt(?),
then climbed the hill overlooking
the town. On top was a woods,
and all through it were the
portable American army
barracks. We were housed in
one. They had rows of cots,
