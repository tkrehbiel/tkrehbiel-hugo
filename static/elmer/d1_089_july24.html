Diary 1 — July 24

The soil seems poor, the
crops as a rule are bad.
On the hilltops the soil
is mostly rock. The
principle occupation seems
to be stockraising; they
have magnificent cattle
and horses here. Amer-
ican harvesting ma-
chinery is conspicuous(?),
and it stands out in
the weather, just as in
the U.S.A.

There is a woods
quiet a way from us; I
mean to see it when
possible. The roads
are splendid, but the
white dirt makes a
motor truck look as
