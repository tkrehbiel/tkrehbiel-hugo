the first time I realized

We were housed(?) in
old French barracks, the
dreariest buildings I
ever saw in my life. But
we didn’t stay there long.
We were registered, then
taken to a smaller barrack.
At night we were routed
out and put through the
mill.

Thursday Jan 16

We were early(?)
loaded onto the
boxcars, 40, no less. And
we didn’t have any(?) too
much rations. We rode
all day, the weather driz-
zly and cheerless.

At night we got ab-
solutely no sleep, there
was not room even(?) to
sit down. And it was
all unnecessary(?), too, for
we could easily have made(?)
the [run] in one day(?).
