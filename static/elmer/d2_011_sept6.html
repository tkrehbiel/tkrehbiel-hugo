Diary 2 — Sept 6

2026_06_01_D2_Aug25 / D2_011_Sept6_IMG_0014.CR3

After mess we went into the
field for close order drill. That
mud stuck to your shoes like
glue and it was impossible
to drill very snappily.

To cap it all, the colonel
came around and ordered
a problem in attack. Another
platoon was the unlucky one.
They had to flop in that
mud.

Friday Sept 6

We on the rifle
range today. Nothing
out of the ordinary happened,
except that 1 fellow blew up his
gun. His barrel was probably
plugged, anyhow, we never did
find the front half of the barrel(?).
