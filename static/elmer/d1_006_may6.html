Diary 1 — May 6

2026_05_25_D1_Apr28 / D1_006_May6_IMG_0004_2.CR3

May 6 Monday

Drill as usual in the
morning. At 11.00 oclock [sic] they
brought us in from the field
and without giving us time to
rest or cool off took us down to
the hospital for our second typhoid
shot. I had to be revaccinated
too, my first didn’t take. I felt
pretty bad just after the shot
but it seemed to wear off. [New pencil.] I
ate a pretty good dinner.

We did no drilling in the
afternoon. They had the bunch of
us in the mess hall and we filled
out blanks about insurance, de-
pendents, military experience etc.
I took out $10,000. Insurance. It
will cost me $6.50 a month.

I felt pretty bad at supper
time. At Retreat I stepped out
of line; I began to see black
