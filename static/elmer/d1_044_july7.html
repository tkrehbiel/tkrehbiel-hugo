Diary 1 — July 7

2026_05_30_D1_May25 / D1_044_July7_IMG_0013.CR3

But today at mess
the first sergeant said,
“Anyone who wants to change
his American money for
French can do so at the
Y.M.C.A.” And the mess
sergeant said “Supper
at 4.45 tonight.”

At 5 we formed outside
with packs. At 7.10 we
left the barracks and marched
to the B.+O. railroad and
entrained. Our train pulled
out at 8.25. At 9._5 [9.05? 9.15? 9.55?] we
arrived at New York and
at 10 our ferry boat took
us to the ship, the
Agamemnon, formerly
the Kaiser Wilhelm.
We got onto the ship
at about 9 o’clock.
