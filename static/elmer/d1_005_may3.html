May 3 Friday

Drilling again, as usual.
I got jumped on continually
so I offered to do a competitive
drill on School of the Soldier with
any man in the company. One
fellow bet me $10 that I couldn’t
do the manual of arms. I wish
I’d had $10. But I took the
sergeants gun when I got back
and did the whole damned manual.

I’m in the kitchen tomorrow.

May 4 Saturday

Spent all day in the
kitchen, scrubbing pots
and pans.

May 5 Sunday

The worst day I’ve spent
yet. Nothing at all to do
and the time takes eternity to
go. Lots of fellows had visitors.
I didn’t.
