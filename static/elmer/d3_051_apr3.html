Diary 3 — Apr 3

2026_06_19_D3_Apr3 / D3_051_Apr3_IMG_0001.CR3

There are about 200 planes there.
I would like to go up in one, but
there is small chance.

Thursday Apr 3

We are now pretty
well settled down.
We have a mess hall, and good
eats. The town too, has two
stores where we can buy things.
And they say that in Grand(?),
just down the road, you can
get anything you want.

Friday Apr 4

Did nothing during the
day, but was put on guard(?)
again for the night.

Saturday Apr 5

On guard. Missed
inspection again.

Sunday Apr 6

A nice day, just
took a walk over the
hills. The country hereabouts
is gently rolling, and well
tilled. I counted 9 teams(?) on
an acre of about 200 acres.
Fortunately the pass(?) evil has
sort of dropped. We can go
to any town in the neighbor-
hood now and nothing is
said. But there is nothing
in any town I’ve seen yet.
