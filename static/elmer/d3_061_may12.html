Diary 3 — May 12

2026_06_19_D3_Apr3 / D3_061_May12_IMG_0011.CR3

It was quite a walk, and
we were good and tired when
we got back.

Monday May 12

[New pencil.] Had an inspection
today. It was in
overcoats! This is only a re-
hearsal for the real inspection.

Tuesday May 13

Another rehearsal in-
spection. I put in my
name for everything new, but I
know I wont get it.

Wednesday May 14

Had our final in-
spection today. I
guess we passed; there was very
little criticism.

Began cleaning up
for a partee. [sic]

Thursday May 15

Got up at the usual
hour. Had reveille and
breakfast. There we all made
packs and began to clean
up. As usual there was a
vast amount of good material
abandoned.

As soon as we got the
billets in shape we assembled(?).
At 10 oclock we marched off.
We joined with the rest of
