Friday July 5

Our barrack bags
went off this morning;
also we practised [sic] getting on
the ship. We are now
sleeping on the springs
of our cots; the mattress
has been emptied and
shipped with the rest of
the stuff.

Saturday July 6

We practised [sic]
getting on the train
today. That and the
inspection was all. We
were allowed out of barracks
after supper, and I went
to the movies for the last
time.

Sunday July 7

We had all
agreed that we
were going to Italy.
