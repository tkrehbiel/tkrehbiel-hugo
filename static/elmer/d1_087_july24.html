Diary 1 — July 24

2026_05_31_D1_July13 / D1_087_July24_IMG_0032.CR3

“Who would like to go to the
company?” I said, “I.” He
said, “Grab this bag of sugar
and take it up.” So I didn’t
get out of it so easily as I
thought.

They said that the vil-
lage was not very far off. I
found that it was. But I
opened that sack when I
got to a lonely stretch and
took toll.

I found the company
quartered in an old village.
I found the billet to which
I was assigned. As I was
so late I expected to find
all the best places preempted
and they were. But there
was a lumberpile in
