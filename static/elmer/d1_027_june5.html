We dug from mid-
night till early dawn,
about 5 oclock. I was
in charge of a squad,
and I think we drew
the toughest piece of
ground within 10 miles.
It was just like concrete
small pebbles tightly
cemented with sand.
The picks were so light
that you had to strike
six times in one spot
to make an impression.
And it was so dark
