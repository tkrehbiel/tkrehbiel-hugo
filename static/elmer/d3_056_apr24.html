Thursday Apr 24

No drill, no work. [maybe !]
We had a hot time
in the barracks last night. The
guard was afraid to come inside.
He yelled “lights out” and let
it go at that.

Friday Apr 25

For our fun last night
our barrack goes on de-
tail today. We dont care, its
cleaning up to go home.

Saturday Apr 26

We had a busy
morning cleaning
up to leave. We dumped away(?)
enough stuff to feed the town,
as usual. Some of the old
women got a wheelbarrowful
of canned willie.

At 9 oclock we began
to move. We had a very easy
march, for we were only carry-
ing light packs; very light;
mine was. The road was
good, the country was pleasant
and the weathere [sic] was fair.
We saw a beautiful chateau
perched on the rim of a
deep wooded canyon.
