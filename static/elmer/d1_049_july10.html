said to the one below him,
“Say Bill, you’d better spread

fully queer.” And I heard
another one say, “Well boys,
I expect to wake up with

Wednesday July 10

When we woke
up, we were all
conscious of a tremor and heave
in the ship, but it didn’t
seem serious. But when we
got onto the floor we found that
it was serious. We were all
drunk in a minute. It
was fortunate that we
didn’t have any clothes to
put on.
