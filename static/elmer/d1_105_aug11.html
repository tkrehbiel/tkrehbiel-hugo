Diary 1 — Aug 11

Sunday Aug 11

I had a pass today
and visited Vaux.
It is a pleasant town, much
better than Isomes. I went
to the Y.M.C.A. there, to church.
It was a good meeting. I
got hold of a book, too; “The
World in Torment(?).” So I had
a very good day.

Monday Aug 12

We started out on
some kind of maneuver
this morning, but after they
had assembled the regiment
it was called off. We just
lay around for two hours. On
the way home they gave
a “Gas” alarm half a mile
from home, and we walked
in with our masks on. I had
a puddle of sweat in mine.
