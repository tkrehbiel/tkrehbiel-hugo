[Teddy, Elmer’s younger brother, is 12 or 13 at the time of writing this. Life sure was different in 1919 Northern Virginia.]

Mar. 9 - 1919
Swetnam, V.A.

Dear Elmer:

This place is sure
a Jonah on horses. Old
Ned fell down in the stable
and couldn’t get up. We
jacked him up a couple
of days and then Walter
walked him out and
shot him. I’m travel-
ing now, Mr. Phipline(?)
says he wants you to
come over and see him
before you doff your
onionform [sic]. We kidds [sic]
set two traps down
along Popes Head.
