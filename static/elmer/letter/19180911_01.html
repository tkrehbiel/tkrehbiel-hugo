Cpl. Elmer H Krehbiel
Co H, 316th Inf.
Am. E. F. N.A.

[Company H was in Tremont on Sept 11, 1918, aka. Trémont-sur-Saulx.]

Sept. 11, 18.
[–]

Dear folks.

It is almost two weeks since I
last wrote you but it was not my fault. We
have moved to the Front, and we get absolutely
not a minute to ourselves. I am writing this
in a YMCA hut, the only dry place in France
today, and will try to get in a few pages before
taps.

You probably wont hear me complain
again that there is nothing to write about. I
wouldn’t have believed it possible to have so
many new experiences in such a short time.
To go back, Sunday two weeks ago we started
out on a divisional maneuver. We got out and
encamped but before we got to work we suddenly
got an order to return to billets. We went in
and made a few preparations but on the whole
the week passed normally. On Saturday we
were out on a battalion maneuver. We went
