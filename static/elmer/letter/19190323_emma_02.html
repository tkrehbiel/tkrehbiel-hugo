Mar. 23, 1919.
441 1/2 Tenn. Ave. N.E.
Wash. D.C.

Dear “Bigs”:

I hope you didn’t faint
when you saw a letter coming
from me once again. I’m a
great letter writer, I am, but
there seems so little to write
about and the folks at
home are writing all the
time I’m pretty apt to get
rather neglegent [sic].

We are all very well
just now. Dick and I
