and two extra bandoliers of ammunition.
We marched 3(?) 1/2 miles in 1 hour 10 minutes,
as I told you before, with all this equipment.
I dont care to do it again.

We are doing no drilling these days,
nothing but infernal and everlasting inspec-
tions. I’ll bet I can recite by heart every
one of the million and one articles that we
are supposed to have. There is one drill,
though, that they put us through; practising(?)
getting onto the steamer. And yesterday we
went to the gas chamber to get acquainted
with gas. They locked us up in a chamber
of tear gas for ten minutes. They made
