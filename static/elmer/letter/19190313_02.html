And as a final clincher, yester-
day we saw a great big flock
of wild geese, or ducks, flying
northward. They were in the
usual wedge formation, one [–]
tail of it having maybe thirty
birds and the other stretching
back five or six times as long.
And they settled one of our pet
arguments for us, for they told
us which was north. Long as we
have been here, we dont know the
directions yet. The sun is so far
in the south that it confuses
rather than helps.

Speaking of lambs, I bet
the kids would like to have one
of those them for a pet. They
are awfully pretty, wooly little
things, clean and white, and lively
and frisky all day. The only thing
I have against them is that
