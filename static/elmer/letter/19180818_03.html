I’ll come back to France, and lie around and

here are the happy hours. The sunsets are
beautiful. So I take a cold plunge in the
creek after “Retreat” and get a book and get
away from the crowd. For that half hour or
hour I’m willing to forget the day.

We feel certain that we are going to
move soon, but I doubt if it will be toward
the front. We might not see that for a
long time yet, and we dont care if we dont.
We have it rough here, but every once in a
while we get a chance to talk with an
Englishman who has been through three or
four years, and that takes all the starch
out of us. But I almost wish they would
let us get in and help wind it up. I think
if they told us we could go home as soon as
we had gotten three Germans apiece we could
be on shipboard inside of a week.

night. In the first place, we get more
