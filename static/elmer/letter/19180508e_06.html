resharpening as the point breaks, and I
would like to have a bottleful of Alophen
pills, like Jake brought home once.

I just got that letter from home. You
certainly got your money’s worth out of
that 3 cent stamp. But I enjoyed all
the letters. Why didn’t Paul send one? And
about half of Carls was questions. I cant
answer them all.

Yours
Elmer.

P.S. Pa, two or three years ago the
Department of Agriculture, or some other
Dept. had a bulletin on the official method
of feed and fertilizer analysis. I wish you’d
get one and have it sent to me. I want
to study up a bit. And forward my
mail. I’m expecting an important letter
from the Bureau of Standards.

We go out to drill in 10 minutes
now so I have to quit.
