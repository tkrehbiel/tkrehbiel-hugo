Aug. 7. ’18

P.S. I forgot to mail my letter last
night, and tonight, since there is no
non-coms school, I have two hours off
so will add a little more and save on
envelopes.

I was reading over what I wrote and
I’ve come to the conclusion that I’ll have to
reenlist after the war, I wont have brains
enough for anything else. I have nothing but
the dimmest recollection of college, I feel more
like a day laborer than anything else. I
sometimes feel that I ought to have put
more push behind my application for a
place as chemist; I would have had some
mental exercise there. I wish I had taken
some of my books along; I suppose you
cant send them, like every other package.
The only reading I’ve done for the last month
is drill books.

Speaking of packages, above, remmets(?) [reminds?] me -
one of our men got a postal card two days
ago from the Adams Express Co., telling him
to call at their Camp Meade office for a package
of fruit.
