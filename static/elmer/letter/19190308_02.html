scrambled out and made for
safety. Another time we saw
three of them, too far off for
a shot. It seemed queer to
me at first but I am assured(?)
that this Argonne forest, which
is in shreds and patches all
about here has plenty of wild
pigs, they call them, and deer
and even wolves.

There is nothing new at
the company, except that we
are to be further reduced in
numbers. About 30 men are to
leave to attend the army trade
schools which are just opening.
And by the way, I’m too late(?)
to enter that university that
they have opened over here. For
that I must thank that hospital
“looie”(?) who said I was going
home.

[Research note: A “looie” is slang for a lieutenant.]
