I have about $130 coming to me now; the
first thing I’m going to do when I get home
is buy a barrel of apples. And I’m certainly
sorry to hear that Billy Sampson is dead.
About the last thing I did when I left
home was sit with him and listen to his
phonograph, and it was one of the first
things I meant to do when I got back.

This matter(?) of [?] getting
my nerves(?): I am used to being broke,
but broke, absolutely, for three months
is a new and painful experience. I
havn’t [sic] had a haircut for about 4
months. Also, I have no razor, I
have to use one belonging to the ward,
and you can imagine how that works.
Still; its good to think of all the
money I’ll have when I step ashore in
the good old U.S.A. I believe that even

[Research note: “H.C. of L.” meant “high cost of living,” a common complaint of the time.]
