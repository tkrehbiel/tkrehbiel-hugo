As you have probably inferred from the
headlines of this outburst, I am not now
in Nevers although we spent several months
there. I’ll tell you the best way to find Nevers.
Take your map in one hand and a pencil in
the other. Punch the pencil for a bull’s
eye right in the center of the map and
there will be Nevers. You won’t have any
trouble finding Verneuil for there are twenty-
six of them. A.P.O. 772 gets me every time.
Three rings and don’t drop the nickel till the
operator tells you. Nevers is a city of 27,000, a
very old town which has several things of
interest, including an ancient temple and
a modern tire factory. Both are very antique.
