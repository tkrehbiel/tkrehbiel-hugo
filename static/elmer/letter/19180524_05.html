On second thought, though, I dont know
whether I’ll be in or(?) not. They are cleaning
out our men right and left, in order to
make room for the new men. Lots of them
have been transferred to the camps at Balti-
more, Laurel, Washington and so on. Several
are being sent to Florida. I havn’t [sic] heard
of me being in line for a transfer but they
give you very short notice out here. One fellow
in my squad went out to drill this morning.
About 10 oclock an orderly came trotting across
the drill field and said, “Private Bedden(?) is
wanted at headquarters.” He left, and when we
came back from drill at eleven he was packed
up and gone, and none knew where to.

In the past two weeks I’ve gotten two
letters. One was a bill and the other was
from Walter asking for the return of his
loan. Is that all the writing you can do?
I’m sending a money order made out to
Pa. The money is meant for Walter. The
