We are offered three
weeks leave to visit England,
if we wish. I would gladly
avail myself of the privilge [sic], for
England is the only country over
here that I care to see, but
there is a huge joker in the
contract. That three weeks
is to include(?) time of travel.
That means that if you are
very lucky you will have three
or four days to spend in
England; but the chances are
about 5 to one that you will
be so long over due at the
company that you will be
charged with desertion.

to Paris. These are only three
