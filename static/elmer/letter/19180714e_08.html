We have been out so long now that
the novelty is all worn off, and we are all
settled(?) down to make the best of it. We
certainly do eat, this air gives a person an
awful apetite [sic]. We all believe we are getting(?)
near to land, or hoping so anyway. The
water here is slatey, it is no longer blue.
The ocean would be alright, if there only
wasn’t so much of it. We have been at
the bottom of a great big saucer(?) all the
time, and we never could climb over(?) the
rim. It certainly is lifeless(?). We have passed(?)
only a sailing vessel, a tramp and a fleet
homeward bound.

We have seen several schools of porpoises.
