But at that that they are better than

The biggest feature of the landscape
hereabouts is an aero station. It is
about two kilos from us. There are about
twenty big hangers and maybe 150
planes; everything from the little Spads(?)
to big, Liberty motored bombers. There
was a captain there who would take
you up in his plane, but he smashed
it to toothpicks the other day so I
guess there will be no more rides. I’d
give a month’s salary/wages/pay to go up once.
They have some German planes there too.
They look awfully cheap and clumsy(?)
alongside the graceful French birds, but
the men there say they are good, swift
fliers.
