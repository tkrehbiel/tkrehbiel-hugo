[?] so utterly busted up that
a goat couldn’t get a living(?) on a thousand
acres of it. Then imagine trenches cut [?] a-
cross through it, and acres and acres of
barbed wire fields strung around. Finally
imagine the whole thing overgrown with
weeds and briars, tall enough to hide
the wire, so it can better trip you up, and
rip(?) your clothes. That is No Man’s Land.
I’ve seen thousands of acres of it, seen it
till the sight of it sickened(?) me.

Somehow(?) all that [illegible] now(?)
I guess if I didn’t have my diary I’d
soon get an Idea that I had dreamed
it. But I dont care; the sooner I forget it
the better I’ll feel.

Here’s hoping I get back before Feb.
1, so I can get in a half year of school
yet.

Elmer

O.K. Censored
