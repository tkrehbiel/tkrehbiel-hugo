Cpl E H Krehbiel
Co H 316 Inf.
Am. E. F.

Apr. 21 1919

Dear folks.

Its me, and I’m still in
Tranpot. I didn’t knock wood hard
enough. The powers were unable to get
enough transportation, so the division
is only moving a regiment a day. We,
of course, are last. So, as matters now
stand, we may get away between Thurs-
day and Saturday. C’est la Guerre.

But its not so bad at
that. We are now doing absolutely
nothing, except standing reveille
and retreat. Soon we will cut those
out and then we will have an ideal
army. And since the rain has finally
stopped (we havn’t [sic] had any for 48
hours) this country is beginning to
look up.
