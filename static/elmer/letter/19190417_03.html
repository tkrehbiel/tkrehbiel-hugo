As for me, Saturday we pull out
from here, going direct to a seaport.
Our colonel said that in 90 days we
would be seasick. It will be the
most enjoyable sickness in the world.
Where we are now we are [–]
rainsick. It is the most miserablest [sic]
misery in the world.

Again I add, dont stop writing. As
for me, I am going to knock wood every
day till we get our discharges, whatever
that is.

Elmer.
