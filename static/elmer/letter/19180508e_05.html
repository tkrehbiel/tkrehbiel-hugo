The YMCA is a great place too. From
6.00 PM on we are at liberty in our own
regimental black. We generally all go to the
YMCA. They have an entertainment every
evening. Last night they had a jazz orchestra
from the 31th, the regiment next to ours,
banjos, mandolins, violins etc. And for
a snare drum they had a pine washboard.
It really fitted in well. They have enter-
tainments too; there’s(?) quite a lot of pro-
fessional actors here. The fellow whose cot(?)
is just at the foot of mine was a comedian
on the Kieth [sic] circuit before he was drafted. He
generally amuses us when we have rests
at drill.
