Such is(?) the army “[illegible]

I got two letters from Esther, one
Sept 27 and one Mar 16. Also, I
got 5 movie magazines and a
Sunday Star. I’ve been busy reading
them so I couldn’t write. But I
find that I still know the stars
by their first name.

I’m writing this letter in the
guardhouse, for I am on guard
again(?) yet. And(?) its(?) time(?) for me
to take out my relief, so I’ll
quit. Now see here: There are 15
sheets to this letter. “Go thou and
do likewise.”

Elmer.

Over

OK. Censored
[no signature]
1st Lt. 316 Inf.
