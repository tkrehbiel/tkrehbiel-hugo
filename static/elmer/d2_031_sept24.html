Diary 2 — Sept 24

2026_06_03_D2_Sept24 / D2_031_Sept24_IMG_0002.CR3

We are being outfitted and
equipped, and lectured to.
It seems troops are being
crowded into all the back
areas. The push will be
a big one.

We had two gas alarms
during the night.

Tuesday Sept 24

Our packs are
made up. We drew
rations today, and am-
munition. We almost ex-

I got three letters from
home today.
