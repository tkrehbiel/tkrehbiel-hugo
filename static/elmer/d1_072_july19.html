and were told to make
our packs; we were
to move at 3 o’clock
in the morning. We rolled
them inside on tents;
there was no dry spot
outside. I made a
nice little pack with
what I had, but I’ll
never be able to put
another blanket on it.

An hour after I
wrote the above we
were ordered to strike
tents and pack up
ready to move at once.
We whooped with joy,
and moved on a jump.
Contrary to expectation,
