Tuesday Aug 27

Today the s??us [sergeants?]
were(?) taken out and shown some-
thing of street fighting. I think
it will be a particularly nasty
branch of the work.

We heard rumors today that
Metz was taken.

Wednesday Aug 28

We went out on
a big regimental field
problem today and, as usual, we
only found out how rotten we
are. Nothing went right, and
the Colonel called off the problem
in disgust.

We got in at 2.30, then were
off the rest of the day. It was
an easy day.
