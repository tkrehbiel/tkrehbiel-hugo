Monday Aug. 5

We began the
day by policing up
the town. We raked the
streets of mud and manure
cut weeds, dug drainage
ditches, etc. The lieutenant
said we would try to get
it clean, but they had ages
start on us! We did make
a big improvement.

In the evening my
squad had its automatic
rifles issued, and we cleaned
them up. I learned to take
them apart; it is an inter-
esting operation.
