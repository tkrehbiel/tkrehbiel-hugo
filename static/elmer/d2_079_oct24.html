marched to a big casual
camp. We stood in line two
hours and got a mess kit and
meal ticket. After eating we
stood in line two more
hours and got a blanket, a
very small one and very light
one, in fact you could put
it in your hip pocket. Then
we were told to bunk in any-
where we found room. But we
didn’t find room, so another fellow
and I bunked on the ground once
again.
