Diary 2 — Oct 17

letters from home.

Thursday Oct 17

The favorite sport
around here now is
getting hold of the daily paper.
It seems that peace is so
near and yet so far. But
I’m surprised to see that
the hospital men(?) are just as
anxious to see the end as we
are. I dont see why, they have(?)
it fine here, I wish I could
look forward to something as
nice.

Friday Oct 18

The day passed as
usual, or as the office
reports say, “There is nothing
to report.”
