Diary 1 — June 15

2026_05_30_D1_May25 / D1_033_June15_IMG_0002.CR3

Saturday June 15

After a field
inspection and
drill I went on pass.

Sunday June 16

On pass at home.
I got in on time
this time.

Monday June 17

I wish to goodness
we’d get on official
designation. One day we will
be called out with the old
men, the next with the
new. Today they had us
out with the new men
doing school of the soldier.

In the afternoon we went
out to shoot for the first
time. But our time was
up before I got my shots
in. We spent the afternoon
on the assault course. It
was hard work; but now(?)
