Sunday Aug. 4

The day passed
quietly for me. We
had inspection in the
morning, then I washed
and shaved up. In the
evening I wrote a letter.
The first mail came into
camp today, but I didn’t
receive any. I went to
church in the evening.

We had one nasty
incident today. Two men
were drinking too much,
I guess, and a fight
started, and ended by
one man getting stabbed
with a bayonet. He will
probably live, but the
one who stabbed him is
