Diary 3 — Feb 17

2026_06_07_D3_Dec6 / D3_034_Feb17_IMG_0018.CR3

The same sort of a town
only better off for stores.
The prices(?) are awful. I
got a box of dates, containing
about 30 dates, for 3 1/2 f.

Monday Feb 17

On the range today.
I find I have a
dandy rifle.

Tuesday Feb 18

No drill today.
We cleaned all
our equipment and rolled
packs for tomorrow.

Wednesday Feb 19

We had a
brigade review
today. It was an im-
pressive sight, only the
mud was too deep for
dignity. But the brigade
and every auxilary(?) was
there and there are several
wheat fields here about which
will never make a crop
next year.

This review I believe
means that something
is about to happen.

Thursday Feb 20

at all to do to-
