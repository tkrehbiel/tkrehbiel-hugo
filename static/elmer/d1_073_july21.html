After our packs were made
up we just stood around. We
kept on standing around. An
hour went by, still we did not
move. The cooks had some
wood there; they built up a
big fire and we crowded around
it. It was a miserable night.
The ground was soaked, we could
not lay down, it rained every
few minutes and a very
chill wind blew. I shivered
in all my clothes.
