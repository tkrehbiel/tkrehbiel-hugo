Diary 3 — Dec 6

2026_06_07_D3_Dec6 / D3_017_Dec6_IMG_0001.CR3

Friday Dec 6

Life goes on almost as
monotonous here. I
go to the mess hall for my meals(?)
now. They give you plenty; but
all the same, I have to go twice
each meal in order to get enough.
All I can do is fill my stomach,
I can’t satisfy my appetite.

I went out on a walk today.
I said I was going till some-
one turned me back. But all
I did was 100 yards down the
road, then I came back and
went to bed. I’m as weak as a
kitten yet.

Saturday Dec 7

Nothing to talk
about.

Sunday Dec 8

I ran across one of
the men from my com-
pany today and he told me
of several others around here.
So today I went to visit one.
I found two on the same ward.
They named(?) about 20 Co H men
in these hospitals. I’m going
to be able to do a lot of(?) visit(?)-
ing when I get strong enough.
