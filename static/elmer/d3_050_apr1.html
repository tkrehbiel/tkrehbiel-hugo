Diary 3 — Apr 1

2026_06_18_D3_Feb28 / D3_050_Apr1_IMG_0015.CR3

At four oclock we arrived(?) at
our billets. They were again
rotten, so we diched [sic?] out and
were immediately jumped on
by the first sergeant. We
had the pleasure of standing
guard that night. But I fooled
him anyway; I would just wake(?)
my man and then get into
his bunk.

Tuesday Apr 1

This morning the
march was easy, but
everyone was so stiff and sore
that it was an awful job.
My right heel tendon was revolting(?)
but I managed to keep up.
[New pencil.] We wound up about noon in the
town of Tranpot(?) in Haute-
Marne, or Vosges, I dont know
which. We were put into good
barracks. The town too, looks
good, so I think we did benefit
by the change. My feet were so
bad that I didnt care to look
around much.

Wednesday Apr 2

Today I went on
a walk to the
aviation field nearby(?).
