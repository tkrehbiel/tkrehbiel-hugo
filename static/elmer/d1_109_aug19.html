Diary 1 — Aug 19

2026_05_31_D1_July13 / D1_109_Aug19_IMG_0054.CR3

Monday Aug 19

The drill is getting
harder and harder. We
had extended order in the after-
noon, hard enough work after a
three mile hike to the drill field.

Tuesday Aug 20

Worse than yesterday.
We went to Cusey in
the morning for gas instruction
and mask fitting. In the
afternoon we went back to the
hill for more extended order. It
is tough, but I realize that it
is necessary.

Wednesday Aug 21

The non-coms went
to Cusey this morning
for bombing practice. We threw
the “OF(?)” bombs. They say they are
not very dangerous, but all the
same I think I’ll be nervous
if I have to run through a
barrage of “OF’s”.
