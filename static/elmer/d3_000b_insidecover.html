[The third diary is also graph paper, but this time Elmer wrote more condensed and filled each line, instead of every other line. The third diary contains the most entries covering the longest time period, from October 1918 to June 1919. Generally the third diary is the hardest one to read because the writing is the most faded, and the paper is very reflective.]
