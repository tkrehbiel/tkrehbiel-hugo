Diary 3 — Jan 8

2026_06_07_D3_Dec6 / D3_024_Jan8_IMG_0008.CR3

Wednesday Jan 8

No fever today.
But they wont
let me get up.

Thursday Jan 9

No fever but still
in bed.

Friday Jan 10

At last they let
me get out of bed.
I was up the whole day. In
the afternoon I was taken up
and reclassified. Also, at night(?)
I was paid, casual(?) pay [?], the(?)
first money I’ve had since
August.

Saturday Jan 11

Today I packed
up and moved on.
The hospital is to be clean-
ed out, only(?) they are send(?)-
ing us back to our outfits, in(?)-
stead of home as they said.
So at about 9.00 oclock I
left the old ward, and that
good bunk, with my whole
earthly possessions in a
little Red Cross bag.

We were given two blankets
and then taken to a
“Chevaux(?) Express.” They
told us to load ourselves
