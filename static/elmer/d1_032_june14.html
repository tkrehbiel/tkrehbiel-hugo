Diary 1 — June 14

2026_05_30_D1_May25 / D1_032_June14_IMG_0001.CR3

Our part was small.
We were in the second
wave, so we had a good
deal of laying on the
ground while the first
wave crept forward. But
it was great fun, and
various(?) too, scrambling
through woods and
swamps with fixed
bayonets.

We had the usual
drills in the afternoon.

Friday June 14

On fatigue detail
today. In the
morning we shoveled(?) clay
packing it down in a stable(?),
in the evening we went
after hay and grain. It
was doggoned hard work.
