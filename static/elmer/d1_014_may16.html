In the afternoon we watched
a ball game between H Com-
pany and E company. Score
12-7, favor H. Later in the
evening, watch a drill
game between 315th Reg team
and 316th Reg team. Score
2-1, in 11 innings, favor 315th.

May 16 Thursday

The usual drill in
the evening. I was
excused in the morning
to get my tooth attended.
I got sick on my stomach
today, for some reason.
The food was good; I can’t
blame it on that. It must
have been the water, the
chlorine was strong today.
