Diary 1 — July 25

2026_05_31_D1_July13 / D1_091_July25_IMG_0036.CR3

and read the articles of
war once again. They had
a rifle inspection too. I,
of course, was out of luck,
for the last time I cleaned
my equipment was on the
boat, and that was some
time ago. My bayonet, in
particular, was crusted deep
with rust. But the officers
were merciful, we didn’t get
a thing.

I hope this will be our
drill field permanently, for
there is a splendid view
from that hill. The walking
though, is poor, for the soil
consists mostly of rock.

In the evening I filled(?)
my bedsack with hay and
made up my bunk good.
