Tuesday Sept 17

I did bunk fatique
all day, only getting
out for meals. It is heaven
after all the drilling we have
done.

Wednesday Sept 18

I had charge of
a police detail in
the morning, otherwise, bunk
fatigue again.

We have frequent alarms
for aeroplanes, and often see
them flying overhead, the puffs
of shrapnel from our guns
dotted all around them.
