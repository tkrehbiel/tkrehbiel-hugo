Friday Jan 17

About noon we
arrived at Suilly(?).
We detrained and stood
around in the rain. There
we were taken out to the
Red Cross hall and sorted
out.

The 316th bunch, about
40, started out. We walk-
ed to Suilly(?), and had a
rest. The walk almost kill-
ed me, for we were carrying
full packs. Later we started(?)
again. I stuck for about
a kilometer, then I fell out.
There was a constant stream(?)
of motor tracks; I knew
someone would pick us up.
And we soon got a lift.

We found the company(?)
at Issoncourt(?), the whole
company in one billet. We
made up our bunks.

Saturday Jan 18

We got reacquaint(?)-
ed and arranged(?).
Also we stood an inspection.
We are back(?) at soldiering(?).
