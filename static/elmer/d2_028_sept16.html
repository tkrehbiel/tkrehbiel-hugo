Monday Sept 16

Today passed just
the same as yesterday.
I happened to think, though,
School is starting in Wash-
ington today; I wonder if
they miss me.

At 7.30 we were told
to pack up. It is the only
place in France that I left
with regret. We started our
march at 11.30(?) and marched
till about 4, then lay down
at the edge of a woods and
slept till dawn. Then we
moved into the woods, into
dugouts that were waiting
