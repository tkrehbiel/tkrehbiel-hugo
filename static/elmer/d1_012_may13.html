day dragged.

For noon today we
had Roast beef and gravy
and dressing, mashed
potatoes, bread, butter and
ice cream, peaches and
lemonade.

Went to church at the
YMCA in the evening.

May 13 Monday

Usual drill in the
morning.

In the afternoon we
drilled till 3, then went for
our third typhoid inoculation.
I was also revaccinated.
We were told to spend the
evening quietly. We did.
I was not sick, though. I
felt rather groggy for the
first hour or so.

Late in the evening
