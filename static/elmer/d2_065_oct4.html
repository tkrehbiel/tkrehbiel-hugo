Diary 2 — Oct 4

But I hope I soon get a uni(?)-
form, this country looks good to
me. My own uniform was taken(?)
away from me at the third
hospital, and for the [?]
days I have been wrapped(?) in
a blanket like an [?] [1:Indian.]

The grub here is fine; we had
canned peaches today.

Friday Oct 4

Nothing to do but lay
in bed and gaze out at
the scenery.

There are six other fellows
in the room with me. Naturally,
until we got acquainted, we talked
war, but the topic soon was dropped.
Two were from Montana, one
from Alaska, one from Lower(?)
California and one from Ohio(?).
