Diary 1 — July 23

2026_05_31_D1_July13 / D1_081_July23_IMG_0026.CR3

Tuesday July 23

The country is
still changing
for the better. There are large
fields and meadows. Evident-
ly they raise beef in this sec-
tion, for we passed many
fine herds. The meadows
here are beautiful; it is one
place in which these farmers
out do the U.S.A. We passed,
too, many vinyards. They
were all small ones, as a
general rule well tended.
But the vines are certainly
planted close together. As
a result of this, perhaps they
are all small, and are all
trimmed up on little stakes.
They do not bear many grapes
but the bushes seem to be
large. They won’t be ripe
