the morning, coming in at 10.30.
We went out at 1 o’clock, to “Cott’s
Goat,” and stayed there till 8 oclock
digging trenches. We carried
full packs.

Saturday Aug 17

We didn’t need
to stand reveille
this morning, the third time
since I’ve been in the army.
In the afternoon we had the
usual drill. It was an easy
day.

Sunday Aug 18

I had a pass today
and I just used it
up in rambling(?) around. The
country farther out looks much
like that close about, only
hillier. I found quite a number
of blackberries, and some
very convenient vinwards(?), too.
