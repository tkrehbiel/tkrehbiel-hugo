It still rained, but

Thursday Sept 12

This morning we
got orders to move
our tents into the woods out
of the view of aeroplanes.
Also, we had gas masks in-
spection, and turned in our
surplus equipment. When we
move we dont know, but it
will probably be soon.

[New pencil.] Late at night we were moved
into town, Fremont. We were
put up in a barn as usual.
