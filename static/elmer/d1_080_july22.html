Virginia. We saw a
farmer plowing. The
horses were hitched tandem.
The plow had a pair of
wheels on the beam, hence
the plowman did not
have to hold onto the
handles at all. They
have a curious way of
plowing. They throw
half a dozen furrows
against each other, then
another half dozen. This
gives the field a corru-
gated appearance. Per-
haps all these d??d [1:dead?]
furrows are necessary
for carrying off the
rainfall, [?] [1:every] field
has them.
