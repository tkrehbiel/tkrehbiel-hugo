Monday July 15

The day passed
without incident. We
heard that one of our ships
rammed a tramp during
the night; I don’t know
how true it is.

I just lay all day
on the deck and slept. For
this reason, perhaps, I
had a severe headache along
in the evening. I didn’t eat
supper, so it disappeared
overnight. The stern(?) of
the ship had a particularly
nasty wiggle and kick. I
guess that helped. It would
be awful to get seasick
so nearly over.
