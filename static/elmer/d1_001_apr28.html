Diary 1 — Apr 28

Apr 28 Sunday [1918]

Reported to local board
Union Station at 8 AM.
Roll was called and we were
dismissed.

Apr 29 Monday

Reported at City post
office at 7.30(?) A.M. Roll
call, etc. Left Union
Station at about 10.00 AM
Arrived at Camp Meade
about 11.00. Marched to
our barracks, about 1 mile
from station, barracks no
T-37. We are attached to
Co. H, 316th Inf.

Our mess kits were served
out and we were given
our dinners, pork and beans
and rice pudding and
coffee. I had always been
told that in the army
