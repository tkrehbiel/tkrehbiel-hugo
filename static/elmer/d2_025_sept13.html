About 7 o’clock we left our
billets. After a little moving(?)
about we were loaded into motor
trucks. Motor transportation
was another of the things we
had looked forward to, and
we were stung again, as usual.
18 men, and all equipment, were
loaded into one small truck.
At the start everyone was
somehow wedged in, and in
that position he remained till
the end of the trip.

It was an 8 hour ride.
We went over a good, very
good road, about 50 feet wide
lined with shattered villages.
