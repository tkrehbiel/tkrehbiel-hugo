Diary 1 — June 29

2026_05_30_D1_May25 / D1_041_June29_IMG_0010.CR3

I don’t like the change
a bit.

We hung around barracks
all afternoon, having various
articles issued to us.

Saturday June 29

Sunday June 30

Went on my
last past [sic] these
days. I didn’t realize till
now that I’m going
to war, but I feel it
now.

Monday July 1

We had all our
equipment laid
out today for inspection, but
the inspectors didn’t get
around to us.

Tuesday July 2

We had our in- [–]
spection this morning
and have been mustered
in. We are now ready to
go across.
