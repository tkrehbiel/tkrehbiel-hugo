May 25 Saturday

There’s a bunch of
sore fellows around
here today. No long
passes are being issued.
They say seven thousand
new men are coming into
camp over Saturday and
Sunday. I know that we
all spent our Saturday
“half holiday” filling bedsacks,
unpacking supplies, etc.

May 26 Sunday

As dismal as
ever. The less
said the better.

May 27 Monday

A very strenuous
day. For the last
four weeks we have
been doing practically nothing.
We were still on School of
