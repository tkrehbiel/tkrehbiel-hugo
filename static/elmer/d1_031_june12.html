Diary 1 — June 12

2026_05_30_D1_May25 / D1_031_June12_IMG_0012.CR3

The time before, I was
afraid to eat much for
supper, and after we got
to work I was afraid
to drink much. But
this time I ate till it
hurt and I drank every
five or ten minutes. My
water bottle was refilled
three times during the
evening.

Wednesday June 12

A day off
for us. I got
up at about 10.30,
the first day we’ve had
a long sleep since we’ve
been in the army.

Thursday June 13

We had battle
practise [sic] this
morning. The whole reg-
iment went out together
