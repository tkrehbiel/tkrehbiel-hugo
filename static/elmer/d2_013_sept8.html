I did go to the pile where the
straw was dumped and lay
down, but I soon found that
it was inhabited. I got out
of there quick.

Sunday Sept 8

We had an early
breakfast, then at about
7.45 we marched out of Isomes
toward Dardeney(?). At Dar-
deney we were joined by the
rest of the battalion and
marched off over a road new
to us. Like every road we
have ever taken, it lead up
hill. Our ration packs were
terrifically heavy, mine
seemed to drag me backward.
