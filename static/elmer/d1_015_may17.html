May 17 Friday

The usual drill and
routine. The drills are
not very stiff, but I felt
it pretty badly, on account of
my bad stomach.

May 18 Saturday

Spent the morning
cleaning the barracks.
The major was doing the
inspecting and everyone was
nervous and excited. We scrub-
bed up everything in sight
and then waited in terrible
suspense. Then came the
inspection. The major walked
into the room, down the center
aisle and then back out!
We almost died when they
told us that was all.
