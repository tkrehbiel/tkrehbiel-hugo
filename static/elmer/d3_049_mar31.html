Diary 3 — Mar 31

2026_06_18_D3_Feb28 / D3_049_Mar31_IMG_0014.CR3

To fry, live coals were raked
out on the hearth and the pan(?)
which had legs three or four
inches long set over them.

We had a supper there, eggs(?)
and fried potatoes. After it
was over, we asked(?) the old man
how much it was. (These were
a dozen eggs and about 1/4 pd(?) of
potatoes.) He said, “Whatever
you want to pay.” We threw
down a 5 franc note for a start
and he said he had no change.
Just to show him how much
we cared(?) we threw down another
5 franc note.

But our bunks were too
good that night. We over
slept and missed reveille.
We couldn’t hear the bugle(?).

Monday Mar 31

This day we had
our longest hike. We
made 32(?) kilometers. We
passed through the area
in which the 88(?) was billeted,
so couldn’t stop in any of
the towns. We had dinner(?)
along the road, one liver(?).
