I had was an hour with

They only ground the sharp

and pulled out the tooth
that Dr. Callahan(?) meant to

May 29 Wednesday

The whole regiment
went on hike to-
gether. And the whole hike
was over concrete roads.
But one good thing; they
served out canteens to us,
so we didn’t have the
awful thirst that we
usually have.

[New pencil.] In the evening
we had a feed, in honor(?)
