Diary 2 — Sept 7

2026_06_01_D2_Aug25 / D2_012_Sept7_IMG_0015.CR3

I and my rifle are becoming
pretty well acquainted now.

Saturday Sept 7

We went onto the
hill today for another
problem. Our company lost
a whole platoon but we reached
our objective. Still it is dis-
gusting work and everyone is
sick of it.

In the evening at retreat
the long expected order to pack
up came along. We discarded
our superfluous equipment,
made squad rolls of 2 blankets
bedsack and overcoat, drew
ammunition and reserve
ration and made up our packs.
We slept on the bare(?) floor.
