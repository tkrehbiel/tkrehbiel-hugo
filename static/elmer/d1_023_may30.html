May 30 Thursday

Decoration day.
We had a regimental
review in the morning, then
in the evening we went out
to watch competitive drills
between picked teams from
each company. I spent the
afternoon marking targets.

May 31 Friday

A good many of
the fellows go on pass
today, so the rest
of us have very little to
do. But in the evening
we went out for an awful
stiff bayonet drill.

June 1 Saturday

Off on a long pass.
Went home to
Virginia.
