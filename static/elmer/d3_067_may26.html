Diary 3 — May 26

2026_06_19_D3_Apr3 / D3_067_May26_IMG_0022.CR3

arrive Thursday.

On guard all night.

Monday May 26

[New pencil.] On guard, probably(?)
the last that the
company will do.

Tuesday May 27

The sea very
calm, the day clear,
cool and pleasant. The old
tub is doing her best now.

Wednesday May 28

We hear that
we are to land
at Philadelphia.

Thursday May 29

This morning,
when I went up for
breakfast, everyone was talking
about the land ahead. I didn’t(?)
believe it but finally ventured
to look out of a porthole. Sure
enough a low sandbank, with
a lighthouse on it, was
visible on the port bow.

I hastily finished break-
fast and went on deck. We
were just finishing our picking(?)
up the pilot. Then we moved
on rapidly. We passed
the Delaware breakwater at
about 8 oclock.
