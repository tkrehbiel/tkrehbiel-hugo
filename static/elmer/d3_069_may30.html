Diary 3 — May 30

2026_06_19_D3_Apr3 / D3_069_May30_IMG_0026.CR3

put onto cars, real passenger
cars, and run(?) out to Camp
Dix, N.J.

Friday May 30

Did nothing all
day. Lots of visitors
in camp.

Saturday May 31

We got deloused
today, only here
they call it deverminized [sic].
The process was not nearly
as good as that in France
and the clothes were steamed(?).
As a result, they were a
horrible mess when we got them.

Sunday June 1

Did nothing again.
They have a good
library and lots of Ys here
so its not hard to pass the
time. But I wish they’d
shoot us through and be
done with it.

Monday June 2

Did nothing again.
Plenty of movies
around here at night.

Tuesday June 3

Again nothing.
