I got a good bunk and slept

noon we had a big roll call,

up and, with a lot of others
I was taken out and loaded

signed up for 2 1/2 francs coffee
money, which was given as a
lump to the sergeant in
charge. None of us ever got
our money, for the sergeant
dropped off at the first big
town.
