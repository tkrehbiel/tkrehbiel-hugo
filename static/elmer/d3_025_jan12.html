Diary 3 — Jan 12

2026_06_07_D3_Dec6 / D3_025_Jan12_IMG_0009.CR3

40 to a car. But we only
had 19. We drew rations
for 40 though.

The train stood till
5 oclock, and in the mean-
time I bought a pound of
chocolates and a pound of
stick(?) candy from the com-
missary. For the first time
since I’ve been in France
my candy appetite is to
be satisfied.

The train pulled out at
5 oclock. I’m glad to get
away, but what am I getting
into?

Sunday Jan 12

The train ran
along all day, [–]
through a flooded count-
ry. This is where all that
water went to.

Also the train runs
much better than(?) they did
in war time.

Monday Jan 13

The first night(?)
we all caught(?) cold,
so last night we made a
careful(?) bunk. We slept
