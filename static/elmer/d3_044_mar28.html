Diary 3 — Mar 28

etc thrown in and enough

At 8.00 we fell in. Of course
we had to stand around for
a while. At 9.15 we left Isson-
court. I at least left with
considerable regret, for it does
not seem possible that we
are going to be much better
off. The weather was cloudy
and cold, but we were soon
comfortably warm from the
exercise.

The first part of the
hike was not bad. After we
got our second wind we went
along easily. The only thing
I didn’t like was taking to
the hill roads, instead of
sticking to the main road.

We did 18 kilos, winding
up, about one oclock in a
little town whose name I
have forgotten, just below
Conde(?). From the first days
experience we were going to
have an easy time(?).
