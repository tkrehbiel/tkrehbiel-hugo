Diary 1 — July 14

2026_05_31_D1_July13 / D1_057_July14_IMG_0002.CR3

I forgot to mention that
in the morning we passed
a big fleet of about 30 ships,
homeward bound. The sailors
said that they were probably
all that we would see, as we
were far off the regular trade
routes.

Sunday July 14

It seems that
during those hot days
we were running up the
Gulf Stream. We are
now out of it. The weather
is very much colder, and
the water has a different
color. It is sort(?) of slaty [sic]
instead of the pure blue
that it was before.

I suppose that I’m
getting used to it, but
life is not so unbearable
