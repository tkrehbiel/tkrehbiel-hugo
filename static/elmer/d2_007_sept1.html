Sunday Sept 1

We had heard rumors
of a divisional man-
euver(?) but noone believed them.
So we were shocked when the
bugles blew first call at 3.05.
We got outside and were told
to get breakfast, then make up
packs for three days in the field.
At 4.30 we were off. We joined
the rest of the battalion at
Dardeney(?), then marched off
eastward, over a road new
to us. It was only a cart-
track leading through a woods.
