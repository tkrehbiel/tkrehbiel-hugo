May 8 Wednesday

Had a hike this morning.
We walked about an
hour, out, then rested an
hour while Lieutenant Johnson
gave us a lecture. Then we
had an hours walk back.
It was a 3(?) mile hike alto-
gether. My left shoe began
to chafe badly toward the end.

Afternoon. Another surprise.
We had been told that Wed-
nesday afternoon was an off day,
we could play ball or anything
we wanted. Now we find that
playing is compulsory. Playing
leapfrog from 1 till 4 in
a 4 pound pair of shoes is
real work.
