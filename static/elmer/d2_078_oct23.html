Then we were marched to
the railroad station. We
boarded a regular passenger(?)
train and in a minute it
was Goodbye Vittel.

We rode till about noon,
then detrained and went(?) to
a casual(?) camp, where we were
given our dinner. [New pencil.] Then we
hung around till 3 oclock,
when we got another meal, a
sufficiently poor one, like the
first.
