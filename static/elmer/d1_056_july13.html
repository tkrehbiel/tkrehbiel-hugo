Diary 1 — July 13

too hot. I sat around
all day and read, curled
up on the deck and slept
and ate three herty
meals. The sea air can
develop a wonderful ape-
tite.

I saw a shark today.
He leaped out of the water(?)
much(?) as the porpoises
do but he was not at
all so graceful.

At night, when we
got into our bunks, we
had a great political
argument. I never laughed
so much in any half
hour in my life.

The night was cool and
I slept like a log.
