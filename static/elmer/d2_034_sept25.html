Diary 2 — Sept 25

This brought us out onto
a good road, and it would
have been easy walking
if it hadn’t been for the
string of supply and
ammunition trucks that
were moving in both
directions.

We marched about 4
hours. The road got stead-
ily worse, loose stones
underfoot, holes, mud and
so on. The woods on each
side began to look pretty
ragged, we thought we
were in no-man’s land.
