May 21 Tuesday

Drilled today in
squads, under(?) cor-
porals from the old company.
I wonder how some of them
managed to get promoted.
I had a date with the
dentist today, but he wasn’t
in. Joy.

May 22 Wednesday

Went on a hike
this morning.
The boys said that it was a
good nine or ten miles, I
said a scant six, the
sergeant said five. In
the afternoon we went through
the usual stupid performance(?)
of watching a ball game.
