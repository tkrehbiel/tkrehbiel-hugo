Wednesday Oct 16

Today we were
examined and
those who had recovered suf-
ficiently were sent away. I
expected to be one of them. My
wound had been dressed three
days before and then left to(?)
recover. But when the doctor
took the bandage off today,
it dripped with pus. So I
am in for a while longer.

It doesn’t make me
mad. I have nothing to
do but walk around and
come back in time for eats.
I’m earning my dollar a day
pretty easily.
