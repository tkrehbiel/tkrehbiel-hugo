Diary 3 — Jan 27

Monday Jan 27

My shoulders
were so sore that
I couldn’t carry a gun at
drill. But they didnt
let me off.

Tuesday Jan 28

Another idiotic(?)
maneuver, chasing(?)
around and flopping
down, we dont know what
for, and then getting ??led [hauled/called]
out for it for doing it wrong(?).

The weather is now
clear and cold, and the
ground is frozen solid.

Wednesday Jan 29

More drill. It
snowed last night
and the ground is white.

Thursday Jan 30

Another man-
euver. But I
got killed, so I didn’t
have much running to
do. My feet got cold though
standing in the snow.

Friday Jan 31

Drill again.
We have inspect-
ion every evening(?) now(?).

Saturday Feb 1

Inspection,
then loafing(?).
