Diary 2 — Oct 19

Saturday Oct 19

Still enjoying
myself as usual.

Sunday Oct 20

We had PIE for
dinner today. It
was only 1/6 of a pie per man;
I could have eaten two whole
ones. Its almost cruel to treat(?)
a man that way.

Monday Oct 21

The awful day has
arrived at last. We
had another examination
today, and I am marked
duty. So its [sic] back to the com-
pany for me I guess. But
they cant send me back
yet with this finger on me.
I cant bend it. [?] [1:Perhaps I’ll]
have to [?] [1:transfer.]
