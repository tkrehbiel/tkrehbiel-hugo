May 7 Tuesday

It’s a very quiet roomful
of fellows. About half of them
are down on their cots with wet towels
on their heads. I don’t feel bad; I
guess I escaped sickness again.

Went for wood at 10.30, a
bad job for such a shakey [sic]
bunch. In the evening we were
taken out to see a regimental
review. I thought it was beautiful.
