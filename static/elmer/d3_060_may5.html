Diary 3 — May 5

2026_06_19_D3_Apr3 / D3_060_May5_IMG_0010.CR3

Monday May 5

Had a practise [sic] in-
spection today.
All shortages were reported.

Tuesday May 6

Nothing doing(?). Went
fishing in the creek
and caught nothing.

Wednesday May 7

Clothing came.
I got what I ordered
and was sorry I didnt order
more.

Thursday May 8

Another inspection.
Otherwise another
walk.

Friday May 9

We heard that the
313 sailed today. Dont
know how true it is, but we
know its near.

Saturday May 10

Nothing at all
to do.

Sunday May 11

Took a walk to
Clisson(?). Its a
beautiful town situated(?)
on one of the prettiest rivers
I’ve ever seen. There was a
castle there, built in 1223.
It is in ruins [–]. We
went all through it, and
bought souvenier [sic] cards.
