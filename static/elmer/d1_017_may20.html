Diary 1 — May 20

2026_05_26_D1_May13 / D1_017_May20_IMG_0004.CR3

After supper four of us
went out and picked straw-
berries. It was rather
early, but we found plenty
of berries.

May 20 Monday

At drill this morning
the sergeant called
me out of the ranks and
told me that my name
had been sent in as rec-
omended for promotion to
non-com. He asked me to
take the platoon for a while
and drill them. I was scared
that they wouldn’t drill
for me so I asked him
to let me try one squad
first. He gave me the tough-
est squad in the company.
But they drilled beautifully
for me, and were better
