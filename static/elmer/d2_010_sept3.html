Diary 2 — Sept 3

2026_06_01_D2_Aug25 / D2_010_Sept3_IMG_0013.CR3

Tuesday Sept 3

[?] had the usual
drilling again. Mostly
problems in attack. We are
still rotten.

Wednesday Sept 4

The usual drill.
Today we got our
rifle grenades and had an
opportunity to see them used.
They are certainly wonderful
things.

Thursday Sept 5

Today, for the
first time in a month
it rained. It actually
rained so hard they took
us off the drill field. So
we sat in the mess hall
and had lectures all morning(?).
