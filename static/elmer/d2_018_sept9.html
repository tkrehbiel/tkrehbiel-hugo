Monday Sept 9

We got up stiff and
sore after a miserable
nights sleep. We broke camp
in the rain and moved off.
Today the order of march was
changed a little. We marched
two kilometers and then rested.
This was very easy marching
and everyone was happy and
contented. We only went about
8 miles, then we arrived at
our entraining point.

The train was not ready
for us so we were marched
into a stubblefield. It was
an awful climb to get there,
and we had to stack(?) a??y
