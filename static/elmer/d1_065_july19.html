Diary 1 — July 19

boarded the lighter. It was
8.30 when we stepped onto
the dock.

The whaff [sic], and the
seawall, and all buildings
in sight, are of old gray
stone, laid in broken courses.
The houses are of the square,
unornamented high pitched
roof sort that we have been
seeing in photographs for
the last five years.

We stood on the dock
for half an hour, then
were marched off at a rapid(?)
pace. We went through the
town. The street was narrow(?)
of coarse macadem(?), and
very much uphill. We
were very gay at first(?).
We sang and yelled at(?)
