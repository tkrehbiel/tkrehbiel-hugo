Friday Nov 1

This morning, after
a lot of standing
around with packs on, we were
placed on motor trucks going
to the front. We pulled out
at about 9 o’clock. Within half
an hour we were in the zone
of the heavy artillery and soon
shells were bursting around us.
We rode(?) into a pocket of gas too.

At about 10.30 our trucks
reached the end of that trip.
We got out and began a walk
up an all too familiar looking
road, a road messed up
with shell holes and broken
wagons and dead horses.
