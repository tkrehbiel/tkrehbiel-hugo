Thursday Aug 1

This was the usual
strenuous day. Then
on top of it I had to be
on guard in the evening. I
slept outside the guardhouse,
it was too stuffy and
stinking inside. As a result
I was stiff and lame all
over the next day.

Friday Aug. 2

This was the easiest
day we’ve had so
far. We had drill till 8
oclock, then a field problem.
I was in charge of a Cossack
post and all I had to do
was sit down on a nice
soft cushion(?) of grass. The
woods here look strange to
me. There is a kind of pine
