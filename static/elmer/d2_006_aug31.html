By “good trees” was meant

The coppice was only bush,
none of it larger than a wrist.
But it certainly grew thick; it
would have been hard work
to force a way through. The
trees were broad leaf, of all
descriptions.

After we got out of the woods
we had dinner, beans. Then
the first three companies
went out, leaving H in re-
serve. We streched [sic] out in
the grass and didn’t move
