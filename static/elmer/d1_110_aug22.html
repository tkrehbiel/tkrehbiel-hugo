Diary 1 — Aug 22

2026_05_31_D1_July13 / D1_110_Aug22_IMG_0055.CR3

In the afternoon we had the
usual drill.

Thursday Aug 22

The usual drill, no-
thing out of the ordinary.

Friday Aug 23

The whole company had
bombing this morning.
We also heard some of the “De
fensing” [sic] bombs go off and those
pieces certainly made music,
whizzing through the air.

In the afternoon we went
back on the hill for a field problem.

Saturday Aug 24

We were on a hike
this morning. Also,
it started to rain. I had seen
it coming, so I had my raincoat(?)
but most of the company was
soaked.

The usual drills in the
afternoon.

Our meals today were fierce.

[End of first diary.]
