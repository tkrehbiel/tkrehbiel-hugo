afternoon.

May 2 Thursday

More drilling. We got
out on the drill field
at 8 and drilled till 10.30,
then we had half an hour
of calisthenics. We drilled
today in groups of about 40.
I thought that my previous
training would make things
go smoothly with me, but its
just the other way. The ser-
geant gives To the Rear, March,
and I do it and collide with
half a dozen behind me who
are just beginning to think of
starting to commence the
movement. Then they all unite(?)
in cursing me for breaking
up the drill.

The grub continues
