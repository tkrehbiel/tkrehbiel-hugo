Saturday Aug 31

We started out
on another battalion
maneuver. We hiked to the top
of the hill, then from there wandered
off into a cart track. We followed
it for a ways, there had a long
fall out, while the officers went
over the ground.

During this time we were
supposed to have our gas masks
on for two hours. After an hour some
one proposed a race between teams
picked from the companies, the
losing company to keep its masks
on for an extra half hour. The
race was run as a relay race,
each team having 8 men. Masks
were worn at the Alert. At
