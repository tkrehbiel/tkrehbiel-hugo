Diary 3 — Apr 7

2026_06_19_D3_Apr3 / D3_052_Apr7_IMG_0002.CR3

Monday Apr 7

Finh(?) tried to stick(?)
me with charge of quarters,
but I rebelled. Now I’m back
at my goldbricking.

Tuesday Apr 8

Nothing to do.

Wednesday Apr 9

They’ve opened a li-
brary now, the best
we’ve had yet. It is a good place
to pass the time.

Thursday Apr 10

Nothing doing, a bad
day.

Friday Apr 11

We got it, officially,
that we are going to
Le Mans “toot sweet.” Its undoubt-
ably [sic] time, they are inspecting
and checking up right and left.
We (the company that is) were
inspected by Gen Kuhn(?) yesterday,
tomorrow we are inspected by
Gen Pershing.

I went to Grand to buy [–]
socks for the hike and missed
another formation.

Saturday Apr 12

Today we got out
at 5.00, had a swift(?)
breakfast and were soon on
the hike. It was a miserable(?)
