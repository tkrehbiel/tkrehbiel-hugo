We marched out, taking
one rest on the way, and
arrived at about 10 oclock.
We pitched shelter tents and
some details went out to
shoot immediately. I was
in the kitchen till late
afternoon, when I got in
20 shots, 10 prone and
10 standing, at 100 yards.
I scored 44 prone and
41 standing.
