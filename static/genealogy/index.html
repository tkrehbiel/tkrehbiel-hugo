Thomas Krehbiel's Blunk, Brown, Krehbiel, Renshaw Photo Home

This is a growing directory of photographs and documents scanned by Thomas Krehbiel from Bob and Gerry (Renshaw) Krehbiel's photo collection. This is an Internet-friendly mirror of the high-resolution TIFF images on my hard drive.

  • Blunks 80 photos

    Blunk family photographs and documents. Relatives and family of Anna May (Blunk) Krehbiel - my paternal grandmother.

  • Browns 440 photos

    Brown family photographs and documents. Relatives and family of Georgennea (Brown) Renshaw - my maternal grandmother.

  • Krehbiels 260 photos

    Krehbiel family photographs and documents. Relatives and family of Peter B. Krehbiel, Elmer Krehbiel, and Robert Krehbiel - my dad.

  • Renshaws 142 photos

    Renshaw family photographs and documents. Relatives and family of Harry Renshaw, Gerald E. Renshaw, and Geraldine Lee Renshaw - my mom.

  • Slides 415 photos

    Bob Krehbiel's slide collection. Krehbiel and Renshaw family pictures from about 1950 through 1975, including Elmer and Anna's 25th anniversary party. Scanned at 1800 dpi with a Plustek OpticFilm 7200 slide scanner.

  • Tin Types 17 photos

    Tin type photographs of unknown people. Most likely either Blunks or Krehbiels. Or Browns. Or anybody else.

  • U.S. Census Records 77 photos

    Census images from Ancestry.com. There may be some copyright issues in distributing these.

  • Unknowns 23 photos

    Photographs of unknown people from Tom's ancestry. Clues are given where available. Otherwise they're anybody's guess.

Photos were scanned at either 300dpi or 600dpi, depending on the quality of the photograph. Slides were scanned at 1800dpi. Documents were scanned at 100dpi or 150dpi. Photoshop enhancements: most images have had their contrast improved. Some color photos were corrected to restore color balance. Some blurry images were mildly sharpened. Occasionally dust, scratches, and blemishes were removed digitally.
