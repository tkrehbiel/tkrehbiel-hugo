Elmer and Anna's 25th wedding anniversary, April 1, 1950. Children and grandchildren of Peter B. Krehbiel. Standing, left to right: Anna, Elmer, Mary Katherine, Jean, Lorraine (Paul's wife), Paul, Carol (Ted's daughter), Esther, John Day (Esther's husband), Alice (Walter's wife), "Punky", Walter, Emma, Richard Boss, Pauline (Ted's wife), Ted. Kneeling, left to right: Bill, John Paul (Ted's son), Bob, Peter (Paul's son), Phillip (Ted's son), Tom (Paul's son). (Identified by Helen Jones, 4/29/2006 - Pauline and Ted confirmed by Debbie Alexander.) (The purple and blue splotches were defects in the slide.) Helen Jones may have taken this picture (reportedly, she was a designated picture-taker) - there are also black & white photos of this pose taken on a different camera.
