Dad wrote "Interesting" on the back of this picture. It appears to be a steam shovel, but I don't know where it was taken or who the people are. It could be from Carroll County (the person in the hat in the steam shovel "door" looks a little bit like John Henry Brown), or it might also be from the logging mill that the Renshaws worked at in Deer Park, Washington.
