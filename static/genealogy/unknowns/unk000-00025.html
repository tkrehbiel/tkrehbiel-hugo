This picture was captioned "Gerald Renshaw and mother," however it was scratched through, so it's not known for sure if this is Amelia and Gerald or not. Date and location unknown. Carol Anglen confirmed Apr 2006 that this is not Amelia Renshaw. It is possible this person has some relation to the Browns, though I can't say how or why, except that the location reminds me of some other Brown pictures. Could this be the woman we see much later in the wheelchair?
