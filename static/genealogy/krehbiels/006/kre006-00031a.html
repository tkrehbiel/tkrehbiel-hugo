Elmer Krehbiel's children and grandchildren after Elmer's funeral, November 1959. Left to Right: Jean Krehbiel holding Cindia (left) and Teena, Bill Jones (behind), Gerry Krehbiel with Richard and Mike, Jimmy Jones, Harold Jones. Taken at Braddock Road house - you can just see the front porch on the right. (Identification help from Donna Tebbetts.)
