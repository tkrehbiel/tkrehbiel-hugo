Standing, left to right: Elmer Krehbiel, Jim Tebbetts, Walter W. Smith (Jim's step-father). Seated, left to right: Mary Katherine, Jean. On the ground, left to right: Anna Krehbiel, Mary Tebbetts (Jim's mother). "These photos were taken during a Elmer and Anna’s only visit to New Mexico to see Mary Katherine and Jim Tebbetts, who were obviously expecting birth of their first daughter, Cindia Jeannette in 1957. These photos were taken in the backyard of James Clarence's mom's home on 5th Street, in Roswell, New Mexico." -Donna Tebbetts
