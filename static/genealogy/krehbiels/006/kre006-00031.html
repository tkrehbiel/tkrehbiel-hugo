Elmer Krehbiel's children and grandchildren after Elmer's funeral, November 1959. Left to Right: Kit holding Teena, Jim Tebbetts holding Cindia, Anna, Helen Jones with Mike, Jean (back), Richard (sitting, front) and Jimmy Jones (behind Richard), Geraldine, Bill (back, behind tree), Harold Jones (front), Bill Jones (front of tree), Bob. Taken at Braddock Road house. (Identification help from Donna Tebbetts.)
