190x Elmer Krehbiel's Fordson Tractor. Dad wrote that this was Elmer's Fordson tractor. It does not appear to be Elmer driving, but it might be Mary Katherine standing on the left and Bob turning the crank. I discovered (5/11/2006) another copy of this picture that was labeled "Indiana Krehbiels" by (I think) Mike and Mary Ann.
