1991 Family picture in Chester living room. Krehbiel family picture taken September, 1991, if my memory serves. Back row, Left to Right: Mary Ann, Mike, Richard, Laurie, David holding Matthew, Jane, Tom holding Chester. Front Row, Left to Right: Stephanie, Geraldine, Adam (in front), Robert with Abby. Taken in the living room of our house at 10747 Surry Road, only a few weeks before Geraldine's death from lung cancer.
