Mathew Blunk (left) and either brother Charles Edwin or Samuel Martin Blunk. April 12, 1942, location unknown but possibly Pope's Head Road property. One copy of the picture is captioned: "Taken the 12th of April 1942. Matt Blunk, Mart Blunk." However, another copy of the picture identifies this person as Uncle Charlie. Scanned from a 3x4.5 gloss print.
