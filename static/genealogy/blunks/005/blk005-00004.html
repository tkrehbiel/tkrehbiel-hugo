Captioned by Dad: "Grandad Blunk, 1934?" Mathew Blunk holding what might be the infamous .410 shotgun. (This shotgun was last known to be in the possession of Uncle Billy.) Scanned from a 3x4.5 gloss print. Dad captioned another 8x10 copy of this print: "Grandad Blunk (Mathew Adrain [sic] Blunk) Early 1930's with the .410 Dbl. barrell shotgun still in the family."
