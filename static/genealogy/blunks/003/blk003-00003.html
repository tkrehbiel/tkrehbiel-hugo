John Kline, date unknown. Captioned: "John Kline." Barely legible: "For Emma Wood." The Klines are linked with the Blunks through James L. Wood's marriage to Emma Kline in 1892. Evangeline Wood later married Lark Blunk in 1915. Scanned from a large cabinet card-style 6"x8" portrait, credited to "Bailey, CCR Sprague and Post, Spokane."
