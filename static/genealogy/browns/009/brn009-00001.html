1892 Possibly Walter Edward and Maggie (Harry) Jenkins with children tintype. This tintype picture was labled WI Jr. 5 weeks. If that is true, it would make the couple Walter Edward and Maggie (Harry) Jenkins, parents of WI Jenkins who would later marry Martha Brown. The other boy's name is unknown. Picture would have been taken around 1892.
