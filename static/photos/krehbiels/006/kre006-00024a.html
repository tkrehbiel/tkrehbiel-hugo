Elmer Krehbiel (left, standing), Walter W. Smith (right, standing - Jim Tebbett's step-father), Anna Krehbiel (left, sitting), Mary Tebbetts (right, sitting - Jim Tebbett's mother). "These photos were taken during a Elmer and Anna’s only visit to New Mexico to see Mary Katherine and Jim Tebbetts, who were obviously expecting birth of their first daughter, Cindia Jeannette in 1957. These photos were taken in the backyard of James Clarence's mom's home on 5th Street, in Roswell, New Mexico." -Donna Tebbetts
