Elmer and Anna Krehbiel German Club picture from George Washington University (this is where Elmer and Anna met). Elmer is standing in the back row, fifth from the right. Anna is sitting, fourth from the left. Aunt Helen related (5/3/2006) that Alice Hoeft, Walter Krehbiel's future wife, is sitting on the front row, far left. Most student names were recorded on a separate paper (kre006-00001A.tif). The dark colors of the print were highly reflective, thus the shiny look.
