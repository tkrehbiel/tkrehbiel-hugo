1957 Bob smoking in kitchen with Gerry. Bob and Gerry Krehbiel, believe it or not. (Dad wrote it on the back of the picture himself.) This picture is interesting because it is so unlike my image of Dad that it is startling. Picture was taken around 1957, I think in Grandpa's kitchen though it doesn't look quite right.
