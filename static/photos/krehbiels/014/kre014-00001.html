1959 Richard Baptism with Revd Bennett at St. Johns. Richard's baptism at St. John's Episcopal Church, 1959. Reverend Bennett is holding him, according to Dad's note. It is funny how he seems to be glowing, as if Richard's baptism was a supremely divine moment.
