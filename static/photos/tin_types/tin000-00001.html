It is conceivable that this is a tin type of Peter and Katie Krehbiel with children Emma, Elmer, and Jake. However, I suspect it isn't. The man and woman seen here do not strongly resemble other pictures of Peter and Katie around this time period. Upon further reflection, the woman on the right slightly resembles the woman in tin000-00013 and tin000-00014, although it's hard to tell.
