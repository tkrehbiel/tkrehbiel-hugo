190x Mysterious Dexter Photograph. This mysterious picture is labeled Ma Dexter. UPDATE 2/12/2008: I just noticed that the old man in this picture strongly resembles Samuel Walker Brown. I also think it's possible that the girl standing on the back row, left is Marjorie, and the girl standing fourth from the left (with the corsage) could be Georgennea or Martha. One of the boys on the left could be Taylor.
