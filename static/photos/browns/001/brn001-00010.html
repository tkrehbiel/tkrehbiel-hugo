Letter from Georgennea to the Army requesting Carl Alfred Marshall be sent home. At this time (April 1919), the AEF troops were languishing in France, not doing much of anything. This letter also shows definitively that Georgennea lived in Kingsport, Tennessee, in the county of Sullivan, on April 28, 1919. It also shows that Carl Alfred Marshall was attached to the Supply Company, 324th Infantry, 81st Division.
